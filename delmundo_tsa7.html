About Stranger Things

Stranger Things is an American science fiction horror drama television series created by the Duffer Brothers. It is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, and follows the story of a group of kids who encounter supernatural events.

Main Characters

Eleven

Eleven, also known as El, is a girl with psychokinetic abilities and a mysterious past. She was raised in a laboratory and escapes to find her place in the world. Her powers are key in the fight against the supernatural forces in Hawkins.

Mike Wheeler

Mike Wheeler is one of the main characters and a close friend of Eleven. He is brave, loyal, and often acts as the leader of his group of friends. Mike is deeply committed to his friends and family, and his determination often drives the group's efforts to combat the supernatural threats in Hawkins.

Will Byers

Will Byers is a young boy who gets trapped in the Upside Down, a parallel dimension. His disappearance sets off the events of the first season. Will struggles with the trauma of his experiences but remains a resilient and integral part of his group of friends.

Dustin Henderson

Dustin is known for his intelligence, resourcefulness, and sense of humor. He often provides comic relief but is also a crucial member of the group due to his scientific knowledge and problem-solving skills.

Lucas Sinclair

Lucas is a brave and outspoken member of the group. He initially approaches situations with skepticism but is always ready to fight for his friends. His resourcefulness and courage often help the group navigate dangerous situations.

Max Mayfield

Max is a tough and independent girl who moves to Hawkins and becomes an integral part of the group. She is brave and resourceful, often showing her strength in the face of danger.
