About Stranger Things

Stranger Things is an American science fiction horror drama television series created by the Duffer Brothers. It is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, and follows the story of a group of kids who encounter supernatural events.

Main Characters

Eleven

Eleven, also known as El, is a girl with psychokinetic abilities and a mysterious past.

Mike Wheeler

Mike is one of the main characters and a close friend of Eleven. He is brave and loyal.
